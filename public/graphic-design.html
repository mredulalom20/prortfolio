FAQ

Common Questions

What is the typical project timeline?

expand_more

Most graphic design projects range from 2 to 4 weeks, depending on the complexity and scope. Identity systems usually take longer than individual collateral pieces.

What file formats will I receive?

expand_more

I deliver comprehensive packages including AI, EPS, SVG for vectors, and high-res PNG/JPG for web use. For print projects, you'll receive production-ready PDFs with bleeds.

Do you offer unlimited revisions?

expand_more

Each package comes with a set number of revision rounds (typically 3). This ensures we stay focused on the project objectives while providing enough flexibility to reach perfection.

What's the investment for a full brand identity?

expand_more

Brand identity packages vary based on scope. A basic logo starts from a competitive rate, while comprehensive identity systems (logo, typography, colors, guidelines, stationery) are priced based on your specific requirements. Let's discuss your needs for an accurate quote.

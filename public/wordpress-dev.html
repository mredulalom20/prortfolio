WordPress vs. Shopify: Which one should I choose?

expand_more

It depends on your business goals. WordPress (with WooCommerce) offers ultimate flexibility and ownership, while Shopify is a powerful, managed platform ideal for scaling e-commerce quickly with less technical overhead.

Can you help with CMS migrations?

expand_more

Yes! I specialize in seamless migrations between platforms (e.g., WordPress to Shopify or vice versa), ensuring your SEO rankings, customer data, and product catalogs remain intact.

How long does a typical custom site take?

expand_more

A standard custom CMS site typically takes 4–8 weeks from discovery to launch, depending on complexity, platform choice, and specific feature requirements.

Do you offer post-launch maintenance?

expand_more

Absolutely. I offer ongoing maintenance packages covering plugin updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and content updates to keep your site running smoothly.

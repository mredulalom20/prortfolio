Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about my design process.

What design tools do you use?

expand_more

I primarily use Figma for the entire design process from wireframing to prototyping. For complex illustrations or high-end visual assets, I integrate Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.

Do you perform user testing?

expand_more

Yes, user testing is integral. I conduct moderated and unmoderated usability tests on interactive prototypes to validate design decisions before moving to development.

How long does a typical UI project take?

expand_more

Timelines vary based on scope, but a standard landing page or small mobile app design typically takes 2-4 weeks from research to final handoff.
